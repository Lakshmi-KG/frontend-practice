As the name suggests, it's a conference-plus-expo for techies and developers who
want to stay ahead of the race by either "working with AI" or "working on AI".

WHY ATTEND?

If you don't want to get obsolete, and want to stay up-to-date with the latest AI-powered tools and techniques related to
development of software and tech, then you must invest a day or two in attending this conference. Plus, you can also figure out
how you can contribute towards developing pathbreaking AI frameworks and platforms like ChatGPT and DeepSeek. Meet the
brightest minds in this field, listen to experts, get up to date, so you're in full control of your destiny and that of your teams.

ABOUT AI DEVCON

AI DEV CON is an attempt by the team at Open Source For You magazine, in partnership with India’s AI community and industry, to bring various
stake-holders of AI eco-system under a single roof. The mission of AI DEV CON is simple: to enable YOU to harnessing the power of AI, and to
enable some of YOU to develop the future of AI.
